Kairat Nurtas

The Pop Icon of Kazakhstan

Biography

Kairat Nurtas (born Kairat Nurtasovich Aidarbekov on February 25, 1989) is a celebrated Kazakh singer and actor. Born in Turkistan, he began his musical journey at a young age, debuting on stage when he was just ten years old.

Over the years, Kairat has risen to become one of the most prominent figures in the Kazakh music industry. His style uniquely blends modern pop elements with traditional Kazakh melodies, creating a sound that resonates deeply with audiences across the country.

Beyond music, he has ventured into film, notably starring in the autobiographical movie "Okinish" (Regret). He is known for his energetic performances, stylish appearance, and a massive, dedicated fanbase often referred to as the "KN Team".
